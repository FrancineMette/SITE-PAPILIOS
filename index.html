About Us

At Papilios Clinic & College, we believe true wellness is more than just the absence of pain, it’s feeling alive, balanced, and at peace.

Our team combines expert hands with a deep passion for healing, offering a range of therapies that draw on ancient wisdom practiced for more than 5 thousands years, such as TuiNa, Cupping, and Reflexology.

Whether you’re seeking relief from chronic pain, a boost of energy, or a moment to simply breathe, we’re here to guide you every step of the way.

Our Founder & Teacher

Behind Papilio’s Clinic & College is Eleanora Bianki, a dedicated therapist and teacher with years of experience in healing arts.

"Since I can remember, I’ve been a healer. I was born with this gift, and throughout my life I’ve continued to study and deepen my knowledge of the human body and natural medicine. Anatomy always came easily to me, and my passion for holistic practices led me to explore Traditional Chinese Medicine and other ancient healing techniques.

Over the years, I’ve combined this wisdom with my own intuition to create a unique approach to healing, one that helps people restore balance, strength, and harmony on physical, emotional, and mental levels.

My greatest joy is seeing someone leave with a smile, feeling lighter and more at peace. I believe that when we are strong and balanced inside, no matter what challenges life brings, we can still find well-being.

With healing hands and a deep intuitive connection, I am able to sense what is happening in your life and guide you through difficult times, helping you release what no longer serves you and reconnect with your inner strength."
